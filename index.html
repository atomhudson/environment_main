Treatment of Wastewater using Membrane Bioreactors

Treatment of wastewater using membrane bioreactors (MBRs) combines biological treatment with membrane filtration. The process involves utilizing microorganisms to break down organic matter in the wastewater, while a membrane barrier separates treated water from biomass. MBRs offer efficient removal of higher quality effluent compared to traditional wastewater treatment methods.

Soil Stabilization with Plastic

Soil stabilization with plastic involves the incorporation of plastic materials into the soil to improve its strength and stability. Plastic fibers or sheets are mixed or placed within the soil, providing reinforcement and reducing its susceptibility to erosion and settling. The plastic helps to distribute loads, increase cohesion, making it suitable for various construction and engineering applications.
